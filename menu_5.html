Preferably I would like the section and gallery to be renovated and have a temporary gallery containing an Air and Space theme. I derived the idea from in the Smithsonian with its National Air and Space Museum down in Washington D.C. As there is a lack of aviation and spacecraft type of Museums or the like within New York City, I thought it would be insightful to have it be shown in the Museum of Natural History. This exhibition will be an inclusive history and a sensory exhibit.
